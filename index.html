El Pollo Loco

Please use Google Chrome for best performance. On touch devices also use fullscreen.

The controls and the goal of the game are explained further below.

Press any button or on mobile touch the game to start it!

The goal of the game is to defeat 'El Pollo Loco' (the crazy chicken).
To achieve this you have to control Pepe, collect the tabasco bottles, avoid the little chickens and at the end of the stage throw the tabasco bottles at the big chicken which is 'El Pollo Loco'.

For keyboard:
Go left: Arrow left Go right: Arrow right Jump: Space Throw Bottle: d
For touch:
Go left: Touch bottom left quarter Go right: Touch bottom right quarter Jump: Swipe up Throw Bottle: Tap top half
